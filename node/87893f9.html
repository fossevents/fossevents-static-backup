Drupal Camp Delhi 2015

  • Conference
Event date: 
Sat, 10/01/2015 - 09:00 - Sun, 11/01/2015 - 18:00

Drupal Camp Delhi (DCD) is the largest watering hole for the Drupal community in India, and the most consistently held Drupal event across the country. It brings together the Drupal developer, business and student user community. DCD is organized by the Indian Drupal community, in association with the Free Software Foundation of India.

DCD Nov’11 witnessed some eminent Drupal personalities like Dries Buytaert, the creator of Drupal and his colleagues Jacob Singh and Ron Pruett - both in senior management positions at Acquia.

DCD Dec’12 was probably the largest gathering of Drupalers in the country. It was also a first time that some of the earliest contributors to Drupal in India from around the country -- Shyamla Rajaram, Dipen Chaudhary, Sumit Kataria, Sumeet Pareek among many others -- were gathered under one roof. For developers and students, the event presented opportunities for learning, getting involved in community contributions via code/ review/ QA sprints for Drupal 7 and Drupal 8, and even find new employers. A lot of trainings/workshops were also organized for the students so they get ample exposure to Drupal and the world of Open Source technology. For the business community there was an opportunity for learning from presentations made on case studies of Drupal websites, meeting representatives of the leading Drupal companies in India, and some of the brightest individuals working on Drupal in the country.

DCD Nov’13 was the 4th event in this series and featured dozens of curated technical sessions, panel discussions and even a day long Drupal Training led by some of the brightest minds of the Drupal and FOSS community in the country. It aimed to unite all Drupal and PHP developers in India to discuss/debate around Drupal and related technologies. 2013 DCD was held on 09 - 10 November and was conducted under the banner of “Free Software Foundation of India”.

About the Organizers
Drupal Camp Delhi is organized by the Drupal Community in India. Contributions from Drupal and Open Source enthusiasts - professionals and students - from all over the country converge to make this event a huge success. DCD’13 was a combined effort of drupal community members.

The aim of the organizers is to educate the technology community about an Open Source CMS - Drupal and provide a platform for developers and students to showcase the work which they have done in this domain. The objective is to also train professionals and students about efficient technology and business practices thus ensuring the creation of a creed of effective solutions practices amongst them. The event is non-profit in nature. The trainings and workshops are absolutely free of cost. The students and professionals attending the event are also not charged for their presence in the premises. This is to make sure that the ultimate goal of the event - knowledge for one and all is met effectively.
