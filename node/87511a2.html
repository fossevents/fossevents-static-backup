About ALUG ( http://angamaly.lug.org.in )

The Angamaly Linux Users Group (ALUG) is a community of Linux enthusiasts from Angamaly area, part of Greater Cochin, India. Our mission is to:

Raise the profile of Linux in Angamaly area.
Be the recognised champions of Linux in Angamaly.
Help make Linux and Linux related software available to local people.
Provide, in cooperation with Indian Libre User Group and other Indian regional LUGs, a resource for Linux users in Cochin and/or links to other resources.
To provide a user group with active, friendly members able to welcome and provide assistance to people entering the Linux world, as well as those who’ve been playing with it for a while.
To hold regular meetings and work to provide Linux related events in the Cochin area.

We eagerly look forward to your valuable presence and active contribution in SFD celebration.
