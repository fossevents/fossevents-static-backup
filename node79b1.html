Open Source Awareness Camp for Girls, Mohali

  • Unconference
Event date: 
Sat, 05/02/2011 - 11:30 - 16:00

Girls generally do not have enough knowledge about the open source world. To make them an active part of the open source world, we are organizing this Free Open Source Awareness Camp for girls at the women engineering college of our campus to encourage open source learning and usage by our college students. This is going to be the first open source event exclusively for girls in the region, and we are determined to this a regular activity in the college to promote open source.

Carte Blanche 2011, Chennai

  • tech-fest
Event date: 
Sat, 05/02/2011 - 00:00 - Sun, 06/02/2011 - 00:00

FOSS Meet NIT-Calicut

  • Conference
Event date: 
Fri, 04/02/2011 - 00:00 - Sun, 06/02/2011 - 11:02

FOSSMeet, abbreviation for Free and Open Source Softwares Meet , is an annual event on Free and Open Source Softwares at National Institute of Technology Calicut. Mukti

  • tech-fest
Event date: 
Fri, 04/02/2011 - 00:00 - Sun, 06/02/2011 - 00:00

Each year Open Source enthusiasts from all over the country rise to it in unison. When Mukti comes calling.... MUKTI is the Annual National Level Technical Symposium on GNU/Linux and Free Software conducted by the GNU/Linux Users' Group of NIT Durgapur. Our sole goal is to promote the awareness of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) in and beyond the student community of India. We have had the opportunity to play host to a number of eminent FOSS contributors at the international level during MUKTI.

conf.kde.in 2011

  • Conference
  • Hackathon
Event date: 
Wed, 09/03/2011 - 14:30 - Sun, 13/03/2011 - 01:30

The KDE® Community is an international technology team dedicated towards creating a free and user-friendly computing experience.

KDE-India is a group of volunteers who are a part of this community, and have been meeting up at various FOSS events in India and abroad. Over the last few years, there has been a substantial increase in contributions to KDE from India to various avenues like coding, localization, marketing, website/infrastructure maintenance etc. There has also been a lot of contributions as a part of programmes like Google Summer of Code and Season of KDE.
