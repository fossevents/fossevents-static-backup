What is Mini DebConf?
Mini Debian Conference

Mini DebConf is a smaller version of Debian Project's developer conference. It is organized by Debian community in a particular region.
Mini DebConf India 2011

We are planning 2011 edition of MiniDebConf India in 4 cities. These events focus on bringing new people upto speed on contributing to debian by bringing existing debian contributers as mentors. Listen, Learn and Practice is our mantra.
